Nepal : 'Also Known as The Land Of The Himalayas'

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia. It is bordered by China to the north and India to the south, east, and west. Nepal is a mountainous country, with the Himalayas running along its northern border. The highest mountain in the world, Mount Everest, is located in Nepal.

Nepal is a diverse country, with over 125 ethnic groups and 123 languages spoken. The official language of Nepal is Nepali. Nepal is a Hindu-majority country, but there is a significant Buddhist minority.
